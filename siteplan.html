Sefa Tees

Professional T-Shirt Brand for Unique Expression

Site Name

Sefa Tees represents a modern, stylish, and customizable T-shirt brand. The name is derived from the founder and conveys a personal, authentic, and creative identity. The website serves as the online store and portfolio for Sefa Tees.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase, promote, and sell custom-designed T-shirts. Visitors can explore available collections, place orders, and learn about the design process. The site also supports community engagement and provides a way to connect with the designer.

Scenarios

  • What types of T-shirts and designs are available for men, women, and children?
  • How can I place a custom T-shirt order or contact Sefa Tees?

Color Schema

The color scheme for Sefa Tees is designed for modern elegance and energy:

  • Primary Color: Navy Blue (#1e3a8a) – used for headers, accents, and navigation bar.
  • Accent Color: Warm Yellow (#facc15) – used for buttons, highlights, and hover effects.

Typography

  • Headings: Georgia – for titles and section headers.
  • Body Text: Segoe UI – for paragraphs and navigation items.
